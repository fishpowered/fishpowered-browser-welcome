Fishpowered browser for Android

Fishpowered is a browser for people who love the web but are tired with all the ads and privacy hassle they see on modern websites, for people who love to multi-task, and for people who love to customise their software to make it their own!

Featuring ad blocker, cookie warning blocker, tracking/tracker blocker, privacy precautions, pinnable windows, gestures, themes, and more coming soon.

The best browser for saving bookmarks, favourites and content?

  • Tap the fave button on any web page, video, or quote and it'll automatically be saved and categorised
  • All your favourites in one place instead of being spread across different apps and logins
  • Optionally tag your favourites to further categorise them

Too busy to read an article or watch a video now?

  • Use the dedicated "save for later" feature to keep track of articles and videos you don't have time to read now

The best browser for customisation?

  • Browser themes
  • Customisable browser buttons
  • Customisable gestures
  • Customisable home page

The best browser for privacy?

  • Fishpowered blocks ads, cookies from external websites and tracking requests by default
  • Enabling Incognito mode allows for 3 levels of further privacy protection from basic to extreme
  • Duckduckgo available as a search engine

Remember when you could use a phone with one hand?

  • Fishpowered browser lets you customise your UI so your most used actions are always in reach.
  • Gestures allow you to perform common actions such as changing and closing tabs quickly and easily.
  • No more hand gymnastics as the most common UI elements are placed in the bottom and middle of the screen.

A useful night mode

  • Instead of just dimming everything we just blacken the backgrounds and lighten the text. It's up to you how much you want to dim the screen.
  • Doubles as a battery saver mode as your screen will be displaying websites as if they all had dark themes.
  • Even works well in daytime if you prefer to read light text on a dark background.

Fast and secure

  • Built using Google Chrome's chromium engine with Google's "Safe browsing" protection built-in
