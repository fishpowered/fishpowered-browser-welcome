Best browser app for language learners?

Here are some features of the fishpowered browser that are great for learning languages…

Translate specific words, sentences and phrases

Learn languages effortlessly by reading your favourite news and sites in a foreign language and translate only the parts you need to.

The translation window is updated automatically everytime you select new text.

Multi-lingual dictionary

Select a word and hit the dictionary button.

With definitions shown in a mini browser window you can easily find related words, etymology etc.

Save your favourite phrases

Fishpowered allows you to save and tag text along with all your other fave things on the web!

And many more useful features...
